What's new vs. today's Reports screen: a real date filter (From / To pickers + Today / 7 Days / 30 Days / Month presets), the full Sales Performance card (success rate, per-method Sales/Returns/Net, six financial tiles) and a Payment Overview card (sales vs returns payments, net payments, collection rate, methods list, donut). The date filter & stylist breakdown map to data the app already has.
