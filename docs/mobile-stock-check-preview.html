Mobile · Stock Check module

Proposed screens for the new Stock Check (physical inventory count) flow on the Astra app, matched 1:1 to the web feature: a count snapshots current stock at creation, then staff count against that fixed list — typing a physical qty or scanning barcodes to +1 — with live variance, and marking items completed reconciles real inventory. Flip the preset and light/dark to confirm parity across every Astra skin.
